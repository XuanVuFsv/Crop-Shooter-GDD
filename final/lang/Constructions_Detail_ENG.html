Construction & Upgrades

🎮 Crop Shooter 📅 Current Version ✍️ For Designers

📋 System Overview

The construction system is divided into 3 main groups. Each building can have its Blueprint purchased at the Builder's Shop, placed on the map, and gradually upgraded over in-game days.

Total Buildings
12
Primary Currency
Granumz (G)
Craftable Gadgets
8 gadgets
Demolish Option
Farming & Lab

Fabricate Gadget Flow

1. Purchase Blueprint at Builder's Shop → 2. Go to Fabricate Gadget and press Fabricate → 3. Gadget is stored (only 1 at a time) → 4. Move to the designated location → 5. Select a placement point on the map.

Garden Water Flow

Pond / Natural Water Sources (salt water) → Water Purifier (filters → fresh water) → Garden (water crops) → Harvest produce.

Farm House Base
📅 Day 1💰 No Blueprint Required
Sleep to skip — skip the remainder of the night and jump straight to 7 AM. Can sleep after clearing all monsters or from 3 AM onward. On wake: fully restores HP and Energy. Movement speed increased by 25% for 3 hours.
⚠️
Sleeping early = losing farming time (Granumz drops, produce, blueprints). Needs balancing so Sleep isn't spammed.
🏪
Farm Market Base
📅 Day 1💰 No Blueprint Required
Sell produce and items for Granumz. Dynamic pricing system using Perlin Noise + supply/demand differential. Selling too much of one type → price drops.
💡
Dynamic Pricing: Perlin Noise combined with sell quantity differential. Selling lots of one type → price drops. Diversify your selling portfolio to maintain good prices.
# Name Effect Cost Day Type
1 Selling Boost +5% sell value on all items (×2 times) Free — Unlock Event

Treasure Pod Base
📅 Day 2💰 Free — fixed placement on map
Scattered at fixed locations on the map, from easy terrain to challenging areas. Rewards: Granumz + Produce + Potion + Blueprint Lv3 Fabricate Gadget.
🎨
Level Design: Clear difficulty gradient. Pod Lv3 must require a high skill ceiling — rewards reserved exclusively for hardcore players.

Builder's Shop Base

🧪 Science Lab — Fabricate Gadgets

Science Lab is the central technology hub, comprising 3 subsystems: Refinery, Fabricate Gadget, and Builder's Shop.

🔧
Refinery: Storage depot — deposit only, no withdrawal. Connect Refinery Link to deposit remotely.
Builder's Shop: Purchase Blueprints with Granumz. Some Lv3 Gadget Blueprints only available from Treasure Pod.
Fabricate Gadget: Stores 1 Gadget at a time, placed on the map at a designated location.
